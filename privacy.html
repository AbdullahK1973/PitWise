PitWise helps drivers understand OBD2 diagnostic trouble codes, save vehicle context, and prepare questions for a mechanic.

Information We Collect

  • App-install identifier: a random ID generated by the app so PitWise can keep your data separate from other users.
  • Vehicle details: make, model, year, engine, and mileage if you choose to provide them.
  • Diagnostic inputs: OBD2 codes, optional symptoms, scan history, and generated guidance.
  • Bluetooth connection data: nearby Bluetooth adapter names and IDs while scanning.
  • Technical data: API requests, server logs, crash diagnostics, device type, app version, and basic usage events if enabled.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide OBD2 guidance, save vehicle and scan history, connect to compatible Bluetooth OBD2 adapters, improve reliability, respond to support requests, and comply with legal obligations.

What PitWise Does Not Do

  • PitWise does not sell personal data.
  • PitWise does not provide a confirmed vehicle diagnosis.
  • PitWise does not replace a qualified mechanic, inspection, roadside assistance, or emergency services.

Data Retention and Deletion

PitWise keeps saved vehicle and scan history until you delete it or until it is no longer needed. You can delete saved data in the app from Settings > Delete My Data.
