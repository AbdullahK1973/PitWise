# PitWise Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: [Insert date]

PitWise helps drivers understand OBD2 diagnostic trouble codes, save vehicle context, and prepare questions for a mechanic. This draft is a starting point for beta testing and should be reviewed before public launch.

## Information We Collect

- App-install identifier: a random ID generated by the app so PitWise can keep your data separate from other users.
- Vehicle details: make, model, year, engine, and mileage if you choose to provide them.
- Diagnostic inputs: OBD2 codes, optional symptoms, scan history, and generated guidance.
- Bluetooth connection data: nearby Bluetooth adapter names and IDs while scanning. PitWise uses this to connect to an OBD2 adapter and read stored diagnostic trouble codes.
- Technical data: API requests, server logs, crash diagnostics, device type, app version, and basic usage events if analytics or crash reporting are enabled.

## How We Use Information

- Provide OBD2 explanations and mechanic-prep guidance.
- Save your vehicle and scan history.
- Connect to compatible Bluetooth OBD2 adapters.
- Improve reliability, safety wording, and app performance.
- Respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

## What PitWise Does Not Do

- PitWise does not sell personal data.
- PitWise does not provide a confirmed vehicle diagnosis.
- PitWise does not replace a qualified mechanic, inspection, roadside assistance, or emergency services.

## Sharing

PitWise may share data with service providers that operate hosting, databases, analytics, crash reporting, support, or app distribution. These providers should only process data for PitWise services.

## Data Retention and Deletion

PitWise keeps saved vehicle and scan history until you delete it or until it is no longer needed to provide the service. You can delete your saved PitWise data in the app from Settings > Delete My Data.

## Bluetooth and Permissions

PitWise requests Bluetooth permissions to find and connect to compatible OBD2 adapters. On some Android versions, location permission may be required by the operating system for Bluetooth scanning. PitWise does not use Bluetooth scanning to track your physical location.

## Security

PitWise uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including HTTPS for production API traffic. No system is perfectly secure.

## Children

PitWise is not intended for children under 13.
