## Bluetooth Adapter Help

PitWise works best with compatible BLE ELM327-style OBD2 adapters. Some low-cost adapters use incomplete firmware or unsupported Bluetooth profiles.

Try these steps:

- Turn Bluetooth on.
- Plug the adapter fully into the vehicle OBD2 port.
- Turn the ignition to accessory/on or start the vehicle if safe.
- Keep your phone close to the adapter.
- Retry scanning from PitWise.
- Disconnect the adapter from other apps before using PitWise.

## Safety

PitWise is a preparation guide, not a confirmed diagnosis. If the vehicle may be unsafe, stop driving when safe and contact roadside assistance or a qualified mechanic.

## Data Deletion

Open PitWise > Settings > Delete My Data. This deletes saved vehicle and scan history for this app install from the PitWise backend.

## Known Beta Limitations
