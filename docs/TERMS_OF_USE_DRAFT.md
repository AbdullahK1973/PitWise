## Service

PitWise provides plain-English OBD2 code explanations, repair-prep checklists, and general vehicle guidance. PitWise is informational only.

## No Professional Diagnosis

PitWise does not provide a confirmed diagnosis, mechanical inspection, safety certification, legal advice, insurance advice, or emergency service. Always use a qualified mechanic or roadside assistance when a vehicle may be unsafe.

## Safety

If your vehicle has brake, steering, overheating, oil-pressure, charging, smoke, fuel smell, severe vibration, transmission, or loss-of-control symptoms, stop driving when safe and contact a qualified professional or roadside assistance.

You are responsible for:

- Deciding whether your vehicle is safe to operate.
- Following applicable road laws and safety practices.
- Using Bluetooth OBD2 adapters safely and only with vehicles you are authorized to inspect.
